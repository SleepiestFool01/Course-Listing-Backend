My Story

Hi! My name is Parker Landes, and I am a senior Cybersecurity student at Oklahoma Christian University. I chose to attend OC for several reasons, but I will highlight three important ones.

Primarily, I wanted to attend a Christian university. My faith is a very integral part of my life, and I wanted to be in an academic space where my professors feel the same way.

Secondly, I knew Oklahoma Christian had excellent Computer Science and Cybersecurity programs. Even before I toured campus, I learned that OC had a nationally accredited program in these areas led by exceptional professors.

Lastly, I chose OC because of the many connections I have here. I am a 3rd generation OC student, and through the extremely tight community this college has to offer, the relationships my predecessors made are still an integral part of their lives. I have come to benefit from these life-long relationships in being surrounded by many family and friends here.

One of my favorite things about Oklahoma Christian is the connection to professors and adults on campus. The faculty and staff are amazing at making themselves accessible and familiar, all for the effort of helping students succeed. I've gone through my four years of college around the same professors, and I feel very blessed to have gotten to know them personally. They always make themselves available, and it's evident they genuinely want all of their students to thrive.
