My Story

Hello, my name is Ryan Allen. I am a Senior here at Oklahoma Christian University, and am currently majoring in Computer Science with an emphasis in gaming and animation. What made me choose OCU was because of the wide variety of programming classes available in my desired field that I could rarely find in other campuses. The friendly community that was introduced to me here helped boost the belief that I chose the right campus, as students and tutors are willing to assist me in nearly any project whenever I had trouble. With all the friends I have made in the past couple years here, my experience at OCU was one of the best I have ever had in my life. I am willing to finish my senior year on a high note and launch myself into a new chapter of my life.

One of my favorite things about Oklahoma Christian is how the community connects to me. Not only are they eager to help others with assignments as I stated earlier, but the events that they host on this campus are certainly one of a kind. I will always remember how much I enjoyed going to a bowling alley with a group of students and professors around my first few days at OCU. But nothing compares to the LAN party that is hosted around the end of each Spring semester, because that is where you can really connect with your peers and enjoy some quality time gaming or chatting. Overall, the community has helped me branch out and form plenty of friendships with the students at OCU.
