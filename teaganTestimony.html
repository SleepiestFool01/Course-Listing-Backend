My Story

Hi, my name is Teagan Minor. I am a Senior here at Oklahoma Christian University. I am currently majoring in Cyber Security. I chose to come to Oklahoma Christian because of its small class sizes and the community feel that it has. I also liked that it was a Christian school because I wanted to go to a school that focused on helping others and giving back to the community. So far, I have enjoyed my time at O.C. and have made some great friends. I am looking forward to the next few years and seeing what the future holds for me.

One of my favorite things about Ok Christian is the professors. They are all very knowledgeable and willing to help students. I have had several professors who have gone above and beyond to help me succeed in their classes. I have also enjoyed the small class sizes because it allows for more interaction
