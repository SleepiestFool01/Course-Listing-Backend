I am a Senior Computer Science major with an emphasis in Cybersecurity. My experiences at Oklahoma Christian University have been some of the best I have ever had. Even through all of the travels I have had the blessings to be a part of, the times I have spent with friends have been second to none.

I would highly reccomend anyone to attend Oklahoma Christian. The top reason I would say this is that the quality of education that is taught at OC would be hard to find anywhere else. With the vast experience and life knowledge that the Proffesors have it is simply immpossible to not learn something new and interesting everyday.

Best Experiences

  • Joining a Social Service Club, Delta Gamma Sigma, to gain a larger friend base and standing on campus
  • Applying and getting accepted onto the Events Tech Team to help contribute to the campus by working large events like Freshman Fanfare and Spring Sing
  • Making friends within my major and my classes to help create study groups and to understand more of what it means to have fellowship at a christian college
