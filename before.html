Before You Arrive

Prep Work for College Life

First Things First

Packing

Bring essentials but don’t overpack. Focus on what you’ll use weekly.

Planning

Get a planner or calendar app to stay on top of assignments and deadlines.

Performing

Set small goals early to build momentum before classes get tough.

Now What?

Arriving

Arrive early to settle in, explore campus, and meet your neighbors.

Meeting Others

Introduce yourself in the dorms and join welcome events to make connections.

Making Friends/Relations

Be open, genuine, and willing to join group activities — friendships will follow.

Go and Conquer!

Classes

Attend lectures, take notes, and don’t hesitate to ask questions.

On Campus Groups

Get involved in clubs or sports to enrich your college experience.

Late Night Shenanigans

Enjoy social events responsibly — balance fun with rest and studies.
